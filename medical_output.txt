According to various sources, including the American Heart Association, Mayo Clinic, and other organizations, the symptoms of a heart attack can include chest pain, discomfort or tightness, shortness of breath, arm, neck, jaw, back or shoulder pain, and more. Women may also experience different symptoms such as unusual fatigue, dizziness, lightheadedness, nausea, vomiting, or sweating. It is essential to recognize these signs and seek immediate medical attention if you suspect a heart attack.
